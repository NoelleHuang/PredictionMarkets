Remember, your understanding of the game and your decisions will affect your payment at the end of the experiment. So to ensure a good amount of payment for you, and data accuracy for the researchers, you should carefully read and understand the rules of the game.

In each session, your group will face a series of prediction tasks (example below) and each of you will take turn to place bets on the respective answers. For each question, you will be given a certain amount of ShruteBuck as currency in this experiment. You may also be given some hints to be used at your disposal; hints received are not to be shared with anyone else. Your ShruteBuck balance at the end of each session will be recorded and added to your account, and later will be used to calculate your cash payment at the end of the experiment. Your ShruteBuck balance will then be reset for the next session.
