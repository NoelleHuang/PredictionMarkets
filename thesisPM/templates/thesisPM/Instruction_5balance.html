At the beginning of each question, you will be given a certain amount of ShruteBuck endowment, which will be used to buy “YES” or “NO” tokens. Each token will pay 1 ShruteBuck (or $1 for short) if it matches the outcome/answer of the question, and will pay $0 when it doesn’t match.

For example, if you have 10 “YES” tokens and 19 “NO” tokens when the question is resolved, then you will have $10 added to your account if the outcome is “YES”; otherwise, you will have $19 added to your account for “NO” Tokens cost ShruteBucks to purchase, and, at any point, the value (number*price) of tokens purchased must not exceed your ShruteBuck balance. You can decide the type of tokens to purchase, and the number of each type of tokens to purchase; however, you cannot decide the prices of each type of tokens.
